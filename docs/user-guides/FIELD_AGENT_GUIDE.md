# Field Agent User Guide

## Overview
This guide provides comprehensive instructions for field marketing agents using the SalesSync mobile application to manage board placements, product distributions, GPS tracking, and commission tracking.

## Getting Started

### 1. App Installation and Setup

#### Download and Install
1. **iOS Users**: Download from Apple App Store
2. **Android Users**: Download from Google Play Store
3. Search for "SalesSync Field Agent"
4. Install the application

#### First-Time Setup
1. **Launch the App**: Tap the SalesSync icon
2. **Grant Permissions**: 
   - Location Services (Required)
   - Camera Access (Required)
   - Storage Access (Required)
3. **Login**: Use credentials provided by your manager
4. **Complete Profile**: Verify your profile information

### 2. Login Process

#### Standard Login
1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Tap "Sign In"

#### Biometric Login (if enabled)
1. Set up fingerprint/face recognition in settings
2. Use biometric authentication for quick access

#### Forgot Password
1. Tap "Forgot Password?" on login screen
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset instructions
4. Follow link to create new password

## GPS Location Tracking

### Enabling Location Tracking

#### Start Tracking
1. Tap "Start Location Tracking" on dashboard
2. Confirm GPS permissions if prompted
3. Wait for GPS signal acquisition
4. Verify location accuracy indicator

#### Location Accuracy
- **Green**: Excellent accuracy (±5 meters)
- **Yellow**: Good accuracy (±10 meters)
- **Red**: Poor accuracy (>10 meters)

#### Troubleshooting GPS Issues
1. **Poor Signal**: Move to open area
2. **Permission Denied**: Check app permissions in device settings
3. **Battery Optimization**: Disable battery optimization for SalesSync
4. **Airplane Mode**: Ensure airplane mode is disabled

### Location Privacy
- Location data used only for business purposes
- Data encrypted during transmission
- Location history available for review
- Can pause tracking during breaks

## Board Placement Workflow

### Overview
Board placement involves installing marketing boards at designated locations with GPS verification and photo documentation.

### Step-by-Step Process

#### 1. Navigate to Location
1. Open "Board Placement" from menu
2. Select assigned board placement from list
3. Tap "Navigate" to open maps
4. Follow GPS directions to location

#### 2. Verify Location
1. Arrive at designated location
2. App automatically checks GPS proximity
3. Must be within 10 meters of target location
4. Green checkmark indicates correct location

#### 3. Select Board Type
1. Choose board type from inventory:
   - Premium Billboard
   - Standard Billboard
   - Street Banner
   - Digital Display
2. Verify available inventory count

#### 4. Take Photo
1. Tap "Take Photo" button
2. Camera opens automatically
3. Frame the board placement clearly
4. Ensure good lighting and visibility
5. Tap capture button
6. Review photo quality
7. Retake if necessary

#### 5. Add Details
1. **Placement Notes**: Describe location and visibility
2. **Installation Quality**: Rate installation (1-5 stars)
3. **Special Conditions**: Note any issues or observations
4. **Estimated Visibility**: High/Medium/Low traffic area

#### 6. Submit Placement
1. Review all information
2. Tap "Submit Placement"
3. Wait for confirmation
4. Commission automatically calculated
5. Inventory updated

### Board Placement Tips
- **Best Lighting**: Take photos during daylight hours
- **Clear View**: Ensure board is fully visible in photo
- **Safety First**: Follow all safety protocols
- **Quality Check**: Verify board is properly installed
- **Documentation**: Include relevant details in notes

### Commission Structure
- **Premium Billboard**: £50 per placement
- **Standard Billboard**: £35 per placement
- **Street Banner**: £25 per placement
- **Digital Display**: £75 per placement

## Product Distribution Workflow

### Overview
Product distribution involves delivering products to customers with signature capture and inventory management.

### Step-by-Step Process

#### 1. Select Customer
1. Open "Product Distribution" from menu
2. Choose customer from list or search
3. Verify customer details and location
4. Tap "Start Distribution"

#### 2. Navigate to Customer
1. Tap "Navigate to Customer"
2. Follow GPS directions
3. Verify arrival at customer location
4. GPS proximity verification required

#### 3. Select Products
1. Browse available product catalog
2. Select products for distribution
3. Enter quantities for each product
4. Verify pricing information
5. Apply any applicable discounts

#### 4. Review Order
1. **Product Summary**: Review selected items
2. **Quantities**: Verify all quantities correct
3. **Pricing**: Check unit prices and totals
4. **Customer Info**: Confirm delivery details
5. **Special Instructions**: Add any notes

#### 5. Customer Signature
1. Hand device to customer
2. Customer signs on screen with finger
3. Verify signature is clear and complete
4. Customer can clear and re-sign if needed
5. Save signature

#### 6. Complete Distribution
1. Review all information
2. Tap "Complete Distribution"
3. Inventory automatically updated
4. Commission calculated (5% of total value)
5. Customer receives confirmation

### Product Categories
- **Premium Products**: High-value items, 5% commission
- **Standard Products**: Regular items, 3% commission
- **Promotional Items**: Special offers, 2% commission
- **Bulk Orders**: Large quantities, tiered commission

### Distribution Tips
- **Verify Products**: Check items before delivery
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Ensure customer is satisfied
- **Signature Quality**: Ensure signature is legible
- **Inventory Check**: Verify sufficient stock before promising delivery
- **Documentation**: Record any issues or special requests

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What if I can't get GPS signal?
A: Move to an open area, ensure location services are enabled, and wait for signal acquisition. If problems persist, contact support.

### Q: Can I work without internet connection?
A: Yes, the app works offline. Data will sync automatically when connection is restored.

### Q: How often are commissions paid?
A: Commissions are paid weekly every Friday, with detailed monthly summaries.

### Q: What if a customer refuses to sign?
A: Document the refusal in notes and contact your manager for guidance.

### Q: How do I report a technical issue?
A: Use the in-app support feature or email tech-support@salessync.com with details.

### Q: Can I see other agents' locations?
A: No, for privacy reasons, you can only see your own location data.

### Q: What happens if I lose my device?
A: Contact support immediately to secure your account and get replacement device setup.

### Q: How do I update my personal information?
A: Go to Profile settings and update your information. Some changes may require manager approval.
